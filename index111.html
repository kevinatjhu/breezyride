About

The Breezy Ride team is an organization that has put serious thought into providing a resource that we believe will help Lyft drivers to avoid situations in which their time is wasted by being stuck in traffic unnecessarily.

This web application is meant to provide the following content to benefit Lyft drivers:

  • Information to help drivers avoid areas prone to traffic congestion. More specifically, research has allowed us to provide predictions of areas likely to become congested in the near future to give insight to Lyft drivers.

  • Information to help drivers avoid areas with a probability of high accidents.
